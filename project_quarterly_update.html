Quarterly Update — Personal Stock Analysis Tool

Problem Statement

Keeping up with quarterly earnings across a personal watchlist spanning multiple exchanges (US, Japan, Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia, Australia) is time-consuming. Each filing runs tens to hundreds of pages. Extracting the metrics, forward guidance, and KPIs that matter — and tracking management forecast accuracy over time — is a repetitive process that does not benefit from being done manually.

Solution

A self-hosted FastAPI application that automates the end-to-end pipeline from earnings filing ingestion to interactive report generation and upload into Google NotebookLM for further querying.

  • 1. Filing ingestion: automatically fetches quarterly and annual filings from SEC EDGAR (10-Q / 10-K), Japan EDINET (yuho / shihanki), and exchanges including ASX, HKEX, SGX, and Bursa.
  • 2. Auto-summarisation: uploads PDFs to NotebookLM as data sources and queries them with YAML-defined structured prompts to extract financial metrics, operational KPIs, and management forecasts from MD&A sections.
  • 3. Report generation: produces interactive HTML reports with Plotly charts covering multi-quarter revenue, EPS, and KPI trends, alongside management forecast vs. actuals tracking.
  • 4. Auto-upload: completed summary reports are re-uploaded to NotebookLM as additional sources, enabling cross-filing queries across the full earnings history of a company.
  • 5. Storage: PDFs and reports are archived to DigitalOcean Spaces (S3-compatible); structured data is persisted in SQLite (local) or PostgreSQL (production).

Tech Stack:

  • Backend: Python / FastAPI, SQLAlchemy ORM
  • AI: Google NotebookLM CLI (nlm) for document querying and summarisation
  • Charts: Plotly (interactive HTML reports)
  • Storage: DigitalOcean Spaces (S3), SQLite / PostgreSQL
  • Deployment: DigitalOcean Droplet, nginx reverse proxy, systemd service
  • Hosted at: 515bedokrsvr.com
Benefits
  • Eliminates manual extraction of earnings metrics across a multi-market watchlist
  • Builds a queryable historical earnings knowledge base in NotebookLM for each tracked company
  • Tracks management forecast accuracy quarter over quarter — a signal that is otherwise easy to lose track of
  • Extensible to any exchange or filing format via modular ingestion services and YAML prompt templates
Evaluation
  • End-to-end pipeline runs unattended from filing fetch to NotebookLM upload
  • Supports 6 exchanges across 5 markets with fiscal year normalisation (e.g. 10-K anchors Q4, preceding 10-Qs mapped to Q1–Q3)
  • Interactive charts and forecast tracking tables generated per company per quarter
