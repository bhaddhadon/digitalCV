Propensity-to-Buy Model: Unsecured Personal Loan

Problem Statement

The first propensity model built at the bank. Branch and outbound call channels needed a more effective way to prioritise Unsecured Personal Loan leads, both within the traditional customer base and beyond it.

Solution

A propensity-to-buy model built and rolled out across branch and outbound call channels, enabling the business to:

  • Pick more effectively from the traditional segment (specific age groups with BAY as primary bank)
  • Tap into non-traditional segments for loan prospects
  • Model: Neural Network

Key Business Objective:

  • Conversion uplift across branch and outbound channels
Benefits
  • First propensity model rolled out at the bank, laying the groundwork for future models
  • Improved targeting in both traditional and previously untapped segments
  • Scaled across two channels — branch and outbound call
Evaluation
  • ~40% uplift in conversion, roughly 1 percentage point increase in conversion rate
  • ~1 million THB incremental revenue per annum
Next Steps
  • Extend the approach to Home Loan and Direct Debit products
  • Roll out to additional channels
