Propensity-to-Buy Model: Term Life

Problem Statement

A bank partner held a large pool of leads for Term Life products, but without efficient prioritisation, the sales channel approached leads with little regard to their actual likelihood to convert.

Solution

A binary classification model built on the bank partner's data to score and select leads for Term Life products.

  • Data platform: SAS database
  • Data preparation & modelling: WPS (third-party software using SAS-like language)
  • Model: Logistic Regression
  • Evaluation approach: A/B testing — model high-score leads vs. a stratified sample across all deciles

Key Model Measurement:

  • Conversion rate uplift vs. stratified benchmark

Key Business Objective:

  • Incremental conversion revenue from bank partner leads
Benefits
  • More efficient allocation of leads to the sales channel
  • Data-driven targeting replacing an ad-hoc, stratified approach
  • Established a reusable A/B testing framework for future lead-scoring models
Evaluation
  • ~40% uplift in conversion, roughly 0.5 percentage point increase in conversion rate
  • ~500K THB incremental revenue per annum
Next Steps
  • Extend the model to other bank-partner products
  • Refine the feature set with more granular partner data
