Next Best Offer

Problem Statement

The direct-to-customer channel on the MyAviva portal relied on generic, manually curated cross-sell offers rather than recommendations tailored to each customer, limiting relevance and engagement.

Solution

A recommender system predicting the next insurance product a customer is likely to buy, built on demographics, policy data, third-party census data and online behaviour.

  • Data platform: Hadoop cluster
  • Data preparation & modelling: Dataiku Data Science Studio (DSS), supporting SQL, Python, R, Spark, Hive and Scala
  • Model: Gradient Boosting
  • Delivery: predicted recommendations served directly on the MyAviva customer portal

Key Model Measurement:

  • Clickthrough rate, pre- vs post-launch

Key Business Objective:

  • Incremental revenue from direct-to-customer cross-sell
Benefits
  • Personalised, data-driven recommendations replacing generic offers
  • Reusable pipeline combining internal policy data with third-party and behavioural data sources
  • Scalable delivery integrated directly into the MyAviva portal
Evaluation
  • ~15% improvement in clickthrough rate, pre- vs post-launch
  • ~100K SGD incremental revenue per annum, with majority of direct-to-customer products being low-ticket-size (e.g. Travel insurance)
Next Steps
  • Extend recommendations to adviser-assisted sales channels
  • Incorporate real-time behavioural signals for fresher recommendations
