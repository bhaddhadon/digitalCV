Smart Leads Allocation

Problem Statement

Turnover time between customers submitting online forms asking to insurance advise and the time they get to speak to advisers can take quite long. Sometimes as long as 5 days due to a manual process of leads assignment. More often than not, product skillset and clientele skillset were also not in consideration, resulting in suboptimal conversion.

Solution

Automation: A work pipeline can be established to assign leads, alert advisers and monitoring their vigilance.

Leads Assignment:

  • Adviser Clustering: K-means clusters on a feature list of product skillset and clientele's demographics skillset.
  • Next Best Adviser: Multi-class classification with product and customer's demographics as features and adviser cluster as a target variable.
  • Adviser ranking within targeted adviser clusters: A business logic of picking individual adviser wihtin a targeted cluster to assign a lead.

Key Model Measurements:

  • Unsupervised adviser clustering: Elbow curve
  • Supervised Next Best Adviser: ~75% Accuracy

Key Business Objective:

  • Leads conversion rate uplift, compared to traditional numbers
  • Turnaround time reduction
Benefits
  • Lower turnaround time
  • Higher conversion rate
  • Better customer experience - adviser with relevant product knowledge and accustomed to advising customer with similar need stage
Evaluation
  • ~80% of turnaround time down to less than 24 hours
  • ~100% uplift in conversion rate translating into >1Mn SGD of incremental revenue
Next Steps
  • Scale the pipeline to all online forms on the company's website/app.
  • Pipeline optimisation: from a scheduled workflow to real-time API call
